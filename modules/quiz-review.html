Reflective Quiz Review

Analyze quiz performance with AI to improve conceptual clarity.

Outcomes

  • Conceptual Clarity
  • Metacognition

Description

Quizzes are not just assessment tools—they can be powerful learning experiences when approached reflectively. In this module, students will analyze their quiz performance by engaging in a structured review process with the help of AI. By revisiting incorrect answers and identifying misconceptions, students reinforce correct concepts and strengthen problem-solving skills.

Activity Flow

  1. Revisit the quiz. For every wrong answer, converse with AI to find out both the correct answer and where you went wrong. Note down these details:
    • What was the error?
    • What was the gap or misconception that led to this error?
  2. At the end, write down the concepts that need to be learned or revised.
  3. What is your plan of action to improve in the next quiz?

The intention is for students to “mark up” or annotate their original quiz. Hence, the deliverable can be the marked up quiz sheets or document.
